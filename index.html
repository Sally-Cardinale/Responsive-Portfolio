About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Sally Cardinale and I am a Full Stack Web Developer (in training)! I have been a local government Program Specialist working in contract support for the past 5 years, and have worked for local government in various capacities for over 15 years.

I am passionate about assisting underprivileged members of my community. It has been a dream of mine to create a non-profit to help children dream big, recognize their potential, and see past their present surroundings.

I am a mom and a wife and I love spending time with my family. We love hiking, game nights and going on scavenger hunts to explore our city. Please explore my portfolio and feel free to contact me if you have any questions, or would like to connect. Have a great day!
